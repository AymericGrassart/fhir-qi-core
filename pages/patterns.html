2.0.0 QI-Core - Patterns

2.1.0 Observation

For observations that have established profiles in US-Core, QI-Core uses those profiles:

Vital Signs Profile"http://hl7.org/fhir/R4/observation-vitalsigns.html"
Smoking Status Profile"http://hl7.org/fhir/us/core/StructureDefinition-us-core-smokingstatus.html"
Laboratory Result Profile"http://hl7.org/fhir/us/core/StructureDefinition-us-core-observation-lab.html"

For all other observations, use the QICore-Observation profile.
